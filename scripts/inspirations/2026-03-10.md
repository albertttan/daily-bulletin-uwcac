### Poem of the Day

> #### <span style="font-size: smaller;">Excerpt from</span><br>On Pleasure
> **Kahlil Gibran**
> 
> &emsp;And now you ask in your heart, “How  
> shall we distinguish that which is good in  
> pleasure from that which is not good?”  
> &emsp;Go to your fields and your gardens, and  
> you shall learn that it is the pleasure of  
> the bee to gather honey of the flower,  
> &emsp;But it is also the pleasure of the flower  
> to yield its honey to the bee.  
> &emsp;For to the bee a flower is a fountain of  
> life,  
> &emsp;And to the flower a bee is a messenger of  
> love,  
> &emsp;And to both, bee and flower, the giving  
> and the receiving of pleasure is a need and  
> an ecstasy.
