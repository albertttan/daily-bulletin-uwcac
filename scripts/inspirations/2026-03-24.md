### Poem of the Day

> #### Poem
> **Run Padgett**
>   
> I’m in the house.  
> It’s nice out: warm  
> sun on cold snow.  
> First day of spring  
> or last of winter.  
> My legs run down  
> the stairs and out  
> the door, my top  
> half here typing
