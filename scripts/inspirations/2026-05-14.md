### Poem of the Day

> #### Snail
> **Langston Hughes**
> 
> Little snail,  
> Dreaming you go.  
> Weather and rose  
> Is all you know.
> 
> Weather and rose  
> Is all you see,  
> Drinking  
> The dewdrop’s  
> Mystery.
