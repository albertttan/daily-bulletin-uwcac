### Poem of the Day

> #### Clouds
> **Rüştü Onur**
>
> Clouds are on the yardarms,  
> Clouds are sailful.  
> Desires are on pomegranate trees  
> Desires are basketful.  
> Yellow roses are on their stalks,  
> Desires are budding  
> Desires are in my unborn child’s  
> Joy of spring.
