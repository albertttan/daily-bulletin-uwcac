### Poem of the Day

> #### sǫ’
> **Manny Loley**
> 
> chahóółhxeel  
> sǫ’ bitsádinidiin  
> nahookǫs bikǫ’  
> binaago deikaah  
> sǫ’ binahjį’  
> hane’ náás oot’ih

> #### Star Poem
> **Manny Loley**
> 
> night drapes  
> stars emit light  
> around the north star-fire  
> stars journey  
> in their wake  
> stories extend
