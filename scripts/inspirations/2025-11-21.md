### Poem of the Day

> #### Hi J, I Decided to Rewrite What I Sent to You
> **E. Ethelbert Miller**
>
> &emsp;*For June Jordan*
> 
> our friendship  
> is what keeps us whole  
> love is essential as air  
> 
> prayer contains desire  
> to worship is to open  
> the door to one's heart  
> 
> we were looking at  
> the ceiling and then  
> we saw the sky
