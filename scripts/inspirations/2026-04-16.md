### Poem of the Day

> #### This Is Just To Say
> **William Carlos Williams**
> 
> I have eaten  
> the plums  
> that were in  
> the icebox  
> 
> and which  
> you were probably  
> saving  
> for breakfast
> 
> Forgive me  
> they were delicious  
> so sweet  
> and so cold
