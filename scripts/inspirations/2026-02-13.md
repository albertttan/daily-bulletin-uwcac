### Poem of the Day

> #### 百人一首 52
> **藤原道信**
> 
> 明けぬれば  
> 暮るるものとは  
> 知りながら  
> なほ恨めしき  
> 朝ぼらけかな

> #### Hyaku Nin Isshu 52
> **Fujiwara no Michinobu**
> 
> Day will be followed by  
> The darkness of night when I shall meet thee,  
> And yet I do hate  
> The breaking of dawn.

**Shared by Rio Fujita.**
