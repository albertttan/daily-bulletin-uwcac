Daily Bulletin

About

The Daily Bulletin project creates Daily Bulletins that are emailed to the students of UWC Atlantic College every morning, providing wellbeing inspiration and helpful information.

The project is a proposal submitted to and implemented by the Wellbeing Council of UWC Atlantic College, with the aim of improving student wellbeing and increasing the Council's visibility. Thanks to all the contributors for making it possible!

The Daily Bulletin project at UWC Atlantic College is also a continuation of the Daily Bulletin project at YK Pao School.
