Daily Bulletin

About

The Daily Bulletin project creates Daily Bulletins to be delivered by email every morning to students of UWC Atlantic College, providing wellbeing inspirations and helpful information.

The project is a proposal submitted to and implemented by the Wellbeing Council of UWC Atlantic College, with the aim of improving student wellbeing and increasing the Council's visibility.

The Daily Bulletin project at UWC Atlantic College is also a continuation of the Daily Bulletin project at YK Pao School.
