Daily Bulletin

About

The Daily Bulletin project creates Daily Bulletins to be delivered by email every day to students of UWC Atlantic College, providing wellbeing inspirations and helpful information.

The project is a proposal submitted to and implemented by the Wellbeing Council of UWC Atlantic College, aimed at improving student wellbeing and increasing the exposure of the council.

The Daily Bulletin project at UWC Atlantic College, which started in February 2025 and is expected to launch in March, is a continuation of the Daily Bulletin project at YK Pao School, which was launched in November 2023 before being partially discontinued since June 2024.
